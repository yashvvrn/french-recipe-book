Mousse au Chocolat

30 minutes + 4 hours chilling
Dessert
Serves 6

Mousse au Chocolat is a classic French dessert that epitomizes elegance and simplicity. This airy, silky chocolate mousse is made with just a few high-quality ingredients, resulting in a luxurious dessert that's both rich and light. Perfect for dinner parties or a special treat, this timeless French classic never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • 200g high-quality dark chocolate (70% cocoa), chopped
  • 6 large eggs, separated
  • 30g unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • 50g granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh berries and mint leaves for garnish
  • Whipped cream for serving (optional)

Preparation

This classic French chocolate mousse relies on perfectly whipped egg whites folded into a rich chocolate base for its signature texture. The mousse needs to chill for several hours to set properly, making it an ideal make-ahead dessert for entertaining.

Chef's Tips

  • Use the highest quality chocolate you can find for the best flavor.
  • Ensure your bowl is completely clean and dry when whipping egg whites.
  • Fold the chocolate mixture and egg whites together gently to maintain the air bubbles.
  • For a more intense chocolate flavor, use 85% dark chocolate.
